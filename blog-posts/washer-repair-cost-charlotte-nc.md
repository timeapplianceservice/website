One of the first questions Charlotte homeowners ask when their washer breaks down: **"Is it worth fixing, or should I just buy a new one?"**

Here's an honest breakdown of washer repair costs in the Charlotte area — from a company that does this every day.

## Average Washer Repair Costs in Charlotte NC

| Problem | Average Cost |
|---|---|
| Drain pump replacement | $150 – $250 |
| Motor coupler | $100 – $200 |
| Lid switch / door latch | $90 – $180 |
| Control board | $200 – $400 |
| Drum bearing | $180 – $320 |
| Water inlet valve | $120 – $220 |
| Full diagnostic visit | $75 – $100 (applied to repair) |

*Prices include parts and labor. Actual cost depends on brand and model.*

## Repair vs. Replace — The Simple Rule

A good rule of thumb used by appliance technicians nationwide:

> **If the repair costs less than 50% of what a new washer would cost — repair it.**

A mid-range washer costs $600–$900 new. So if your repair is under $400, fixing it almost always makes financial sense — especially for quality brands like LG, Samsung, Bosch, or Whirlpool.

## Which Brands Cost More to Repair?

**Higher repair cost (but worth it):**
- Miele, Bosch, Electrolux — European parts cost more but these machines last 15-20 years
- Speed Queen — parts are pricier but the machines are built like tanks

**Most affordable to repair:**
- Whirlpool, Maytag, Amana — parts are widely available and inexpensive
- LG, Samsung — common parts are readily available

## Common LG & Samsung Error Codes We Fix Daily

Charlotte homeowners with LG and Samsung washers frequently call us for:

- **LG LE error** — motor issue, usually fixed same day
- **LG OE error** — drain problem, often a clogged filter
- **Samsung 5E/SE** — drain error, pump or filter
- **Samsung dC/DE** — door error, latch or wiring
