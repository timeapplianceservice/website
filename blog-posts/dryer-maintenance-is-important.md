A lot of customers only call when the dryer completely stops working. But regular dryer maintenance can help prevent expensive repairs and even reduce fire risk.

One of the most common problems we see is heavy lint buildup inside the dryer and vent system. This restricts airflow, causes overheating, increases drying time, and puts extra stress on parts like the heating element, thermostat, rollers, and motor.

Signs your dryer may need maintenance:
• Clothes taking too long to dry
• Dryer getting too hot
• Burning smell during operation
• Loud noise or vibration
• Dryer shutting off mid-cycle
• Excess lint around the machine

We work on all major brands including LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, Maytag, GE, Kenmore, Frigidaire, Electrolux and more.

Regular maintenance helps your dryer:

✅ Dry clothes faster
✅ Use less electricity or gas
✅ Last longer
✅ Work more safely
✅ Prevent costly breakdowns
