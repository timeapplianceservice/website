90-Day Warranty Policy

Our commitment to standing behind every repair.

90 DAYS

Warranty on Parts & Labor — Every Repair

What's Covered

Every appliance repair performed by Time Appliance Repair comes with a 90-day warranty covering:

  • Parts: Any part we install is covered for 90 days from the date of repair
  • Labor: All labor performed on the repair is covered for 90 days
  • Return visits: If the same issue recurs within 90 days, we return at no charge

What's Not Covered

  • New, unrelated appliance failures after the original repair
  • Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or external factors (power surges, floods, etc.)
  • Issues resulting from failure to follow the technician's post-repair instructions
  • Repairs performed by other companies after our service

How to Make a Warranty Claim

To make a warranty claim, simply call us at (980) 990-2911 and reference your original service date. We'll schedule a return visit promptly.

We stand behind our work. If we made a mistake or something we fixed fails again within 90 days, we make it right — no questions asked.
